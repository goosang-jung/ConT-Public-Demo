# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that participation in this project will be a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of level of experience, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

✅ **Respectful Communication**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Listening to different viewpoints and experiences
- Providing constructive feedback

✅ **Collaborative Spirit**
- Working together toward shared goals
- Acknowledging the contributions of others
- Helping teammates succeed

✅ **Professional Conduct**
- Being honest about capabilities and limitations
- Admitting mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on the best solution, not the best idea

✅ **Inclusive Mindset**
- Making space for marginalized voices
- Welcoming questions and curiosity
- Celebrating diverse perspectives

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

❌ **Harassment & Discrimination**
- Slurs, insults, or discriminatory comments
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Threats or intimidation
- Discrimination based on identity characteristics

❌ **Hostile Communication**
- Trolling, flaming, or deliberate provocation
- Personal attacks or ad hominem arguments
- Deliberately derailing conversations
- Exclusionary language

❌ **Abuse of Platform**
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Spam or mass messaging
- Sharing others' work without attribution
- Violating others' intellectual property

❌ **Professional Misconduct**
- Deliberate spread of misinformation
- Sabotaging others' contributions
- Taking credit for others' work
- Inappropriate content in any form

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

📧 **Email**: conduct@conception.ai  
Include:
- Description of the incident
- People involved
- Timeline
- Any supporting evidence

💬 **Discord**: Mention a moderator or use `/report`

**All reports are confidential and taken seriously.**

### Response

Upon receiving a report:
1. Acknowledgment within 24 hours
2. Investigation within 3-5 business days
3. Appropriate action per severity
4. Outcome communicated to reporter

### Consequences

Violations may result in:
- Warning or clarification request
- Temporary suspension (24 hours to 30 days)
- Permanent ban from community
- Reporting to hosting platform (GitHub, etc.)

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub, Discord, forums)
- Public spaces where someone represents the project
- Private communications related to project business

## FAQ

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**  
A: We value growth. If you make an unintended mistake, apologize and learn.

**Q: Is the Code of Conduct enforced equally?**  
A: Yes. Regardless of contribution level, status, or influence.

**Q: What if I disagree with enforcement?**  
A: You can appeal to the Community Team at conduct@conception.ai.

**Q: Is this Code of Conduct legally binding?**  
A: No. It's a commitment to community values. Legal issues are handled separately.
